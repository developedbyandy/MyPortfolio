About Me

There's an interesting story about how I fell upon developing software. After graduating from Rowan University in 2014, I went to work in telecommunications where I eventually became a store manager for a cellphone company. After working in sales for about 4-5 years, I quit my job and went over to work for iHeartMedia as an account executive.

The Covid-19 pandemic put the world on pause and put me out of a job. I started driving uber to pay the bills while my best friend, who is a software engineer, started to talk me into learning how to code. I had nothing better to do at the time as I was out of a job, so I took some free classes online to teach me html and css, and then on to javascript. I caught the bug pretty early and caught an attraction to designing websites.

One thing lead to another and I found out about Thinkful, and started a prep course with them to start my journey into becoming a software engineer, and I couldn't be happier! If you're looking to hire a new developer or just looking for a friendly partner to work on projects with, please contact me! I'd be happy to work with other developers on building an amazing software.
